Sicily

The Mediterranean's biggest island is separated from the mainland by the Strait of Messina.
A mountainous spot, Sicily's coast and its small islands sit at the foot of volcanoes, including Etna, Stromboli and Vulcano.
Surround by water with a warm climate its a beach lovers dream.
